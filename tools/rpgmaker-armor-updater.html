⚔️ RPG Maker MZ Armor Updater

Rescale armor values and prices based on parameters and traits

How it works:

  • Upload your Armors.json file from your RPG Maker MZ project
  • Armor values are rescaled based on their actual parameter bonuses (positive values only)
  • Prices are adjusted with random variation (±20% by default)
  • Prices end with "00" (e.g., 78100)
  • Base price increases for each trait the armor has (excluding Ex Parameter: Evasion Rate)
  • Pricing: 1000 gold = 10 euros
