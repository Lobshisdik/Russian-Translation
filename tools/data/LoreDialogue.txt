* Tower makes you think about scale and perspective. We're just ants looking up at infinity.

* Represents humanity's relationship with the incomprehensible. A monument to cosmic indifference.

* Some days it looks like hope reaching toward heaven. Other days like despair falling from space.

---

* Children adapt faster than adults. They treat impossibility like playground equipment.

* Tower divides people into believers and skeptics. Both groups are equally wrong and right.

* Maybe the real mystery isn't where it came from but why we're so surprised it's here.
